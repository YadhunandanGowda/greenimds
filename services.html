BOM assessment

We Review BOM And Perform Gap Assessment. Data Collection Will Be Performed Only For The Required Or In Scope Parts. For In-House Parts, We Create Data For Keeping Material Data Or Drawing As A Reference.

Data Collection/Data Validation

Data collection from suppliers will be planned for in scope parts as per process. Data collected will be reviewed against respective Directive or Regulatory requirement. Extensive follow up is done till data is collected from supply chain.

Risk Assessment

Parts for which suppliers cannot provide data, risk assessments can be performed to qualify parts provided we get material data or drawings or data sheets etc.

Product Compliance Statements

Based on data available, product compliance statements are generated as per directive or regulatory requirements.
